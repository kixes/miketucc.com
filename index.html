Pamela's Story

"Four years ago… They say diet and exercise cures everything. Nice theory. If only it was just that easy!! Surprisingly enough, you don’t wake up heavier/fat overnight. How does it happen? Well it’s all about prioritizing. I work a few jobs by choice. As demands increase, I would make myself available to work all the time. That’s when I put on weight. I know what to eat or not… basically how to exercise. What I had forgotten was how to make a few hours for my own health. That’s when I thought to employ someone that could help me fit in fitness on a regular basis. Point being, working out includes strength, cardio and flexibility training to feel great and achieve your goals.

Training with Mike Tucciarone for the past four years has taught me it’s all about lifestyle changes. Putting in time with Mike and some days on my own. You don’t have to be perfect just put some effort in on a regular basis."
